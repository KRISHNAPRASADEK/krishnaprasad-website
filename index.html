Project

APPLICATION FOR ONLINE TO OFFLINE SERVICES

A utility android app is designed to service customers on their move to provide locality based services with more user friendly interfaces.... The app offers immediate solutions to mechanic service, food service, medical service, electric and electronic services and many other services which is vital in present world as we mainly dependent on tertiary sector. When the user is in need of a service, the app furnishes a list of nearby service providers from which the user can select. Address of service provider are also made available to the user with the help of this app along with review ratings.
The choosen provider is automatically intimated for his or her service requirement and the customer is given necessary assistance. This app benefits small shops by giving recognition which eventually increases the availability of jobs and the number of good entrepreneurs for the growth of our country. The customer can also provide ratings to the servicer, which in return ensures the originality of the service provider.
